Company Overview

Integrated Decision Systems Consultancy (IDSC)
specialises in the provision of advanced analytics insights to business decision makers through consultancy and other augmented intelligence services

With over 20 years of experience, we have developed two of our consulting solutions into automated Demand Forecasting and Resource Optimisation engines

These engines are delivered through Analytics-As-A-Service and embody the know-how of scarce talent to allow businesses to harness the benefits of data analytics in a faster and less costly way.

The company, founded by Mr Sim Cheng Hwee, has been providing advanced analytics solutions since 1996 to clients in public sector, manufacturing, healthcare and other commercial sectors.

OUR MISSION

To harness the power of decision technology such as Advanced Analytics, AI and Augmented Intelligence to help clients distill their corporate wisdom into explicit models in order to:

- make intelligent decisions consistently and coherently

- promote better communication

- learn and accumulate knowledge as a corporate body over time

- gain and maintain a competitive edge
